Topic Brief: Side of the Czech Republic

     The American Mafia, an Italian-American organized-crime network with affairs in cities all over the United States, especially New York and Chicago, became powerful by dominating the illicit liquor trade of the Prohibition era in the 1920s. After the Prohibition era, the Mafia adopted more criminal endeavors, from illegal gambling to drug trafficking, and also corrupted legitimate business and labor unions, such as New York’s Garment Industry and construction.
     The fight for influence amongst the Mafia was one marked with violence and scandal, further impacting every aspect of society, politics, and culture throughout America’s rapidly increasing industrious cities. From the violent Castellammarese War to the diplomatic creation of the Commission, organized crime in the 1920s was being mediated by the American Mafia across America, particularly in New York City. As a member of any one of the several crime-families throughout the nation, it will be your responsibility to maintain a loyal relation with your family and uphold the core value of the Mafia: Omertà. With the emergence of a newly formed Commission, many issues and crises will emerge and it will be up to you to find a solution to stop them, whether that be by adhering to the “code of silence” or ‘fessing up to the feds!
