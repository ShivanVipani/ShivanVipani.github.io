WHO

     East Bay Model United Nations is an annually held event that invites hundreds of delegates from all over the bay area and beyond to participate in a weekend of debate, education, and growth. The conference, held at the award-winning California High School, is run by high school students ensuring a personal and unique spin on the MUN format. This website will be updated regularly with further details. Through the combined efforts of students from Foothill High School from Pleasanton and California High School from San Ramon, this conference is able to continue to provide an enriching and engaging experience for its third consecutive year.
