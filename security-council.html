Topic 1: Israel-Palestine Conflict

     The UNSC is tasked with the daunting task of proliferating international peace and security, and also is the only council permitted to deploy UN peacekeepers. After its creation post WW2, committee is made up of 5 permanent world powers as well as 10 rotating seats. It is now time for its delegates to delve into the ongoing tensions between Israel and Palestine. With official territorial disputes between the two entities dating as far back as 1964, the ideological separation between the Judaic and Islam citizens cause massive feedback in the form of destruction and violence. At the heart of this issue are the conflicting claims over Jerusalem, A major holy site for all Abrahamic Religions, which provoke further hate, segregation, and hostility with each passing day.


Topic 2: Extrajudicial Killings in Counterterrorism

     In direct contention with the established due process of legal jurisdiction within the UN, Extrajudicial killings are unauthorized elimination or neutralization of a specific target or threat by a political entity. In the interest of public safety countries may pose to the security council to suspend an individuals civil rights in order to bring peace and the greater good, but even with UN approval, civilian casualties may still needlessly come about. The current controversy surrounding the issue is the use of drones in middle eastern nations and other unstable regions.
